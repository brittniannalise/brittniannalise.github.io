About Me

Hi there! My name is Brittni Helms and welcome to my blog! Whenever a friend asks me for my go to meals, they are always shocked to hear that I seldomly cook the same dinner twice. Our family is about as adventurous as they come with our foods - they say that variety is the spice of life and I like it spicy!

On this blog, I will share my adventures in shaking things up in the kitchen and I hope it will spark a flame in yours as well! Every few days I will add a new recipe and the ones I post will run the gamut, from the savory to the sweet, the indulgent to the healthy, and from the comfy to the audacious. Also, my Instagram stories are usually filled with clips about the recipes I am making every day, so follow me so you can ask questions and cook along if you'd like!

To tell you a bit about myself, I was born in Anchorage, Alaska, and raised in a small town in Utah. When I married my husband, who was practically raised in a kitchen, the only cooking story I had involved undercooked chicken. After six years, literally moving from coast to coast to coast, two kids, and seven years of school, cooking is now my jam! While my husband might want to take the credit for teaching me everything I know, I got it all from the internet, so now is my chance to share what I've learned.
